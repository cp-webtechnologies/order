Our Story

St Cyprian Restaurant was founded in 2010 by Nuwagaba Cyprian, who had a vision to create a restaurant that serves high-quality food in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

Over the years, we have grown to become one of the top-rated restaurants in the city, known for our exceptional service, delicious food, and cozy ambiance.
